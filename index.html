Total Population and GDP across South Asian Countries

By Lexi Lutz

Looking at South Asian GDP and population growth over the past few years, we see a large disparity. When analyzing the population differences and the GDP gap between countries there is a correlation between the size of the economy (measured in GDP) and the growing population. From this we can predict the growth of the economies in each country and see the link between population spikes and thriving domestic economies.
